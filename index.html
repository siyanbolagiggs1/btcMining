Progress So Far

   

  • (2021)
    Humble Beginnings

    We began mining at home with a used Antminer S9 from a Chinese seller on ali express.
    Thanks to cheap electricity!

  • (2022)
    Scaling the Operation

    We secured a $3,000 investment fund, bought two Innosilicon T2TH miners, & moved operations to a permanent industrial site with stable electricity.

  • (2023)
    Building a Trusted Network

    Gridless announced a partnership deal with us to deploy 10 miners at our hydro facility.
    This marks our first international collaboration to expand Bitcoin mining in Africa.

  • (2024)
    An Agency is Born

    Steady profits and new partnerships transformed the venture into a registered Bitcoin mining agency with a group of dedicated teams across the country.

  • (2025)
    Transition to Full Service

    The agency has expanded beyond bitcoin mining and now offers full Bitcoin infrastructure services beyond hosting. E.g, consulting & energy deals.
